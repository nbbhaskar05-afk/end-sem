Section A strategy (10 × 1 mark)

All 10 questions are compulsory. These cover short definitions, 1-step numericals, and concept identification. Focus on: unit definitions, formulae recall, functional group identification, battery chemistry one-liners, smart material definitions.

Section B strategy (attempt 4 of 5–6 questions)

Each question is 5 marks (recent format). Choose questions covering different units to maximize coverage. Always attempt the spectroscopy question (Woodward-Fieser + IR numerical — guaranteed marks). The Michaelis-Menten derivation + R/Rmax numerical repeats every year.

Section A: 20–25 mins (read all, attempt all). Section B: ~20 mins per question × 4 questions = 80 mins. Buffer/revision: 25 mins. Prioritise numericals first within each question — partial marks are awarded.
