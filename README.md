A dark-themed, single-file HTML study tracker built for 
B.Tech 2nd Semester Spring 2026 end semester exams.

Features:
- Syllabus checklists for all 6 subjects with topic-level tracking
- Live progress bars per subject and overall completion %
- Exam countdown showing days left to each exam
- Stats dashboard — topics done, remaining, subjects completed
- Progress auto-saves in browser (localStorage)
- Works offline — no internet needed after download

Subjects covered:
- Transforms & Numerical Methods (MA11002) — Apr 13
- Chemistry (CH10001) — Apr 15
- English (HS10001) — Apr 16
- Creativity, Innovation & Entrepreneurship (PS10045) — Apr 17
- Basic Electrical Engineering (EE10002) — Apr 18
- Basic Electronics (EC10001) — Apr 20
